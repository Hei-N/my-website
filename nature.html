the village

~ day one, no signal ~

Nature won. Took the train two hours out of Tokyo and ended up in Hinohara, an actual village inside Tokyo Prefecture (who knew). My phone gave up trying to find a signal somewhere around the second mountain.

"Hinohara village 001, Hinohara, Tokyo, Japan" / CC BY-SA 4.0, via Wikimedia Commons

The next day I went further out, all the way to Shirakawa-go. Different prefecture, but the same feeling - tiny wooden houses, snow on the roofs, smoke from the chimneys. It looks like a storybook.

"Shirakawa-go (2014-06-03)" by Alpsdake / CC BY-SA 4.0, via Wikimedia Commons

It's quiet in a way I forgot was possible. Just leaves, wind, my own loud footsteps.
